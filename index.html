Sachin Tendulkar

The God of Cricket

Sachin Tendulkar after 2011 World cup win

Here are some of the biggest achievements of Sachin Tendulkar in his 23 year career:

  • Debut at the age of 16 on Indian tour of Pakistan.
  • Arjuna Award recipient for achievements in Cricket.
  • Padma Shri – India's fourth highest civilian award.
  • ICC 2010 Cricketer of the year – Highest award in the ICC listings.
  • First person to score a double century in One Day Internationals.
  • Total tests played 200 and highest score in tests 248.
  • Highest number of centuries (49). Highest number of fifties (96).
  • Sachin Tendulkar has played the most one dayers (463 ODIs).
  • Most Man of the Series (MoS) awards (15).
  • Sachin Tendulkar has scored the highest number of centuries in the history of Test cricket (51).
  • With 49 centuries in ODIs and 51 in tests, he scored a total of 100 centuries in his entire cricket career.
  • Till date, Sachin Tendulkar remains the only player in the world to have scored 5 Test centuries before the age of 20.
